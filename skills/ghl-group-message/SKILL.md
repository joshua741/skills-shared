# How to Create a Group Message in GoHighLevel

This skill walks through the exact steps to create and send a group SMS in GoHighLevel. The sub-account and sending phone number are fixed — participants and message content are provided at runtime.

## Fixed Values

- **Sub-account:** Webber Wealth Holdings
- **Phone number:** +18065459009

## Procedure

1. Log into GoHighLevel. From the Agency Dashboard, click **"Click here to switch"** in the top-left corner and select **Webber Wealth Holdings**.

2. In the left sidebar, click **"Conversations."**

3. At the top of the conversations panel, click the **pencil/compose icon** (below the Snippets tab, just above the message flow list).

4. In the **"Create New Message"** popup, click **"Group Message"** (the middle option — *Send group SMS to contacts*).

5. In the **"Create New Group Chat"** window, click the **"Phone number"** dropdown under *Select your phone number* and choose **+18065459009**.

6. Click the **"Search by contact name or number"** field under *Select participants* and search for and select the specified contacts. Minimum 2 participants required, max 10 total (including yourself).

7. Click the blue **"Create Group"** button. The group conversation opens automatically in the main chat window.

8. Click inside the **"Type a message"** box at the bottom and type the message.

9. Click the blue **"Send"** button to send the group message to all participants.

## Notes

- Participants and message content are not hardcoded — they should be specified each time this procedure is used.
- GoHighLevel limits group messages to 10 participants max (including yourself), with a minimum of 2.
